IPD & Emergency running 24x7 round the clock with a bed strength of 40 beds, one model Labour Room, Blood Storage Unit (BSU), Sick Newborn Stabilisation Unit(SNSU)
General OPD running 9am to 2 pm everyday (Except Sunday and some fixed holidays) including Ayurved OPD, Dental OPD (Everyday), EYE OPD (Every Wednessday, Friday & Saturday) with other line department like X-Ray, ECG, ICTC.
