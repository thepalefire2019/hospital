SERVICES PROVIDED BY US

SERVICES

IPD & Emergency

IPD & Emergency running 24x7 round the clock with a bed strength of 40 beds, one model Labour Room, Blood Storage Unit (BSU), Sick Newborn Stabilisation Unit(SNSU)

OPD Service

General OPD running 9am to 2 pm everyday (Except Sunday and some fixed holidays) including Ayurved OPD, Dental OPD (Everyday), EYE OPD (Every Wednessday, Friday & Saturday) with other line department like X-Ray, ECG, ICTC. Laboratory (Sputam & Malaria), Anwesha Clinic (Every Monday, Wednessday, Friday & Saturday)

ANC Clinic

Ante Natal Clinic (ANC) held 3 days in a week in presence of Medical Officer (G & O), Health Supervisor, GNM, ASHA at Bagnan RH (Every Monday, Wednessday & Friday), & All Three PHCs (Adra, Bainan, Mankur) 1 day in a week

Cesarean Delivery, Mini Lap & NSV operation

Cesarean Delivery & Mini Lap held at Bagnan RH (Every Tuesday, Thursday & Saturday), & NSV operation held on every Thursday.

RCH Plus Camp

Outreach camp held in presence of Medical Officer, Health Supervisor, ANM, ASHA at Haturia-I GP SC (Every Wednessday), Kalyanpur GP SC ( Every Friday) Khajurtti SC ( Every month 2nd Wednesday)

Laboratory Test

Govt Lab

At Bagnan RH & Mankur PHC ( Malaria, Sputam for AFB, Blood Sugar, HIV Test)

PPP Lab

Doctors Laboratory at Bagnan RH Campus (All kind of test done including USG)

Measures taken for prevention of Vector Borne Disease

Regular review of Dengue prevention activities at BLMT by The BDO

Training of all Medical Officer, ANM, ASHAs and all other paramedical staff has been completed.

IEC material displayed in every ward , emergency, OPD and other key areas at Bagnan RH, also at PHCs and Sub Centres

Hospital and Block level monitoring team visit different health facilities, private institution to ensure and monitor dengue preventive and health activities
